About Us

TechNova Solutions is a premier IT services and consulting company founded in 2010, headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small startup of 5 people to a global team of 500+ engineers, designers, and business analysts.

Our mission is simple — to deliver reliable, scalable, and innovative technology solutions that help businesses grow faster and smarter. We have successfully completed 1200+ projects for clients across 35 countries including USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and Germany.

We are proud partners of Microsoft, Google Cloud, and AWS, which allows us to offer our clients the best-in-class infrastructure and tools. Our ISO 9001 certified processes ensure that every project we deliver meets the highest standards of quality and security.

At TechNova, we believe that technology is not just about code — it is about people. Our client-first approach means we listen before we build, and we support long after we launch. That is why 85% of our clients return to us for their next project.
